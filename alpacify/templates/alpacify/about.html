Everybody knows about the zombie apocalypse. Everybody knows what will happen. Everybody has a plan on how to survive, and most importantly we all know what happens when you get bitten by a zombie. But what do we know about the Alpacalypse? We know that it, along with the zombie and raptor apocalypse, will happen. Sooner or later, the alpacas will spread like wild fire across the globe. But did you know that alpacas work the same way as zombies; when you're bitten by an alpaca you become an alpaca.

This knowledge is something that the crew of WonderLAN shared with us, and it got us thinking; how would we look if we were alpacas? So we created the website that you're currently visiting, AlpacifyMe. It lets you upload an image of yourself, and by the power of our highly sophisticated software it will replace your face with that of an alpaca.

So how does this fantastic application work? It's easy! Simply follow this link, choose a picture and click "Alpacify Me!". Then all that's left to do is to wait for the results.

Good luck with the Alpacapypse!
